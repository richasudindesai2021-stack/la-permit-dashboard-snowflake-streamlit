## â­ Key Findings

### **1. Luxury home upgrades are concentrated in wealthy ZIP codes.**

Pools and high-valuation projects cluster in areas such as 90049, 90210, and 90272.

### **2. Kitchen renovations spiked after 2020.**

Average valuations and permit counts increased significantly during the post-COVID remodeling wave.

### **3. Renovation inequality is strongly tied to income.**

High-income tracts produce more permits than their share of households; lower-income areas lag behind.

### **4. A few contractors dominate the construction ecosystem.**

One contractor alone completed over 132,000 projects, demonstrating structural imbalance in renovation activity.

### **5. Citywide construction trends show long-term growth.**

Major permit types (additions, alterations, new builds) increased steadily with a COVID-era dip.
